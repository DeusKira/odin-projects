Stuffed Peppers

Credits: The inn at the crossroads

Description

“Seven courses were served, in honor of the seven gods and the seven brothers of the Kingsguard. The soup was made with eggs and lemons, the long green peppers stuffed with cheese and onions.” -A Dance with Dragons

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1 (8 ounce) package shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
  • 1 onion, minced
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground cayenne pepper
  • 15 fresh jalapeno peppers, halved lengthwise and seeded
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 tablespoon milk
  • 2-3 cups crushed corn flake cereal

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Lightly grease a medium baking sheet, or line with foil.
  2. In a medium bowl, mix together cream cheese, cheddar cheese, onion, and spices. Stuff jalapeno halves with the mixture.
  3. Whisk together eggs and milk in a small bowl. Place crushed corn flake cereal in a separate small bowl.
  4. Dip each stuffed jalapeno half into the egg and milk mixture, then roll in corn flake cereal to coat.
  5. Arrange in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet. Bake in the preheated oven 30 minutes, or until filling is bubbly and lightly browned.
